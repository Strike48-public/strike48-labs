Pick is the connector marketplace for the Strike48 ecosystem. It integrates with StrikeHub to let you discover, install, and manage connectors.

## Prerequisites

- **StrikeHub** installed and running
- **Internet access** to reach the Strike48 marketplace API

## Accessing Pick

Pick is available as a connector inside StrikeHub. It appears in the sidebar alongside your other connectors.

1. Open StrikeHub
2. Click **Pick** in the sidebar
3. Browse the connector catalog

## Installing a Connector

1. Browse or search the marketplace catalog
2. Click on a connector to view its details
3. Click **Install** to download and register the connector
4. The new connector appears in StrikeHub's sidebar

## Updating Connectors

Pick checks for updates to installed connectors. When an update is available:

1. A badge appears on the connector in the Pick catalog
2. Click **Update** to download the new version
3. Restart the connector to apply the update
