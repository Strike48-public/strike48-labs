Pick is the connector marketplace for Strike48. It provides a catalog of available connectors, handles installation and updates, and integrates with StrikeHub to manage your connector library.

## What It Does

- **Browse connectors** — Discover available Strike48 connectors from a curated catalog
- **Install and update** — Download connector binaries and keep them up to date
- **Manage your library** — Enable, disable, and configure installed connectors
- **StrikeHub integration** — Installed connectors automatically appear in StrikeHub's sidebar

## How It Works

Pick connects to the Strike48 marketplace API to fetch the connector catalog. Each connector listing includes:

- Name, description, and icon
- Version history and changelog
- Binary downloads for supported platforms (macOS, Linux)
- Required StrikeHub version

When you install a connector through Pick, it:

1. Downloads the binary for your platform
2. Registers it in `~/.config/strikehub/connectors.toml`
3. Notifies StrikeHub to refresh its connector list
